Biriyani

Description:

Biryani is a popular and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and typically some type of protein like chicken, beef, lamb, fish, prawns, or vegetables. The dish is known for its aromatic and rich taste, often resulting from a blend of various spices, including but not limited to saffron, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves, coriander, and mint.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken/Lamb
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic Paste
  • Chili Powder
  • Bay Leaves
  • Cloves
  • Black Peppercorns

Steps

  1. Marinate Protein
  2. Cook the Rice
  3. Prepare the Meat/Vegetables
  4. Layering
  5. Dum Cooking
