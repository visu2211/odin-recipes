Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that has become a global culinary phenomenon. It consists of a round, flattened base of dough topped with various ingredients and baked at a high temperature.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Add Sauce and Toppings
  3. Bake the Pizza
